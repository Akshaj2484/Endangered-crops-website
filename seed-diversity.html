Why Seed Diversity Matters

Preserving Crop Diversity

Genetic Diversity

Seed diversity ensures a wide range of genetic traits, making crops more resilient to climate change, pests, and diseases.

Food Security

Maintaining diverse seeds ensures a stable food supply, even when certain crops fail due to environmental stress.

Cultural and Nutritional Value

Many traditional crops have unique flavors, nutrients, and cultural significance that are preserved through seed diversity.

Environmental Benefits

  • Reduces dependency on chemical inputs
  • Supports pollinators and biodiversity
  • Maintains healthy soils
