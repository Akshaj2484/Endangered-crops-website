About Endangered Crops

Preserving Crop Diversity

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about rare and endangered crops worldwide. We aim to preserve agricultural biodiversity, protect cultural heritage, and ensure sustainable food security for future generations.

Why It Matters

Endangered crops are vital for genetic diversity, climate resilience, and nutrition. Losing them can make our food systems less adaptable to climate change and more vulnerable to pests and diseases.

How We Help

  • Promoting seed-saving initiatives
  • Educating farmers and communities
  • Collaborating with research institutions
  • Highlighting rare crops online to increase awareness
